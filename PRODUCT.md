# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Medium-term individual investor using a quiet Windows desktop companion to keep portfolio concentration, holding logic, and risk signals visible without turning the workflow into short-term trading.

## Product Purpose

Position Logic Pet helps the user observe configurable holdings, related price signals, announcements, margin heat, and portfolio concentration through fixed observation formulas. It explains whether the holding thesis still looks intact, why a reminder appears now, and which variables deserve review. It does not predict next-day price moves or give mechanical buy/sell instructions.

## Brand Personality

Calm, vigilant, grounded. The interface should feel like a patient risk companion: active enough to notice events, quiet enough to avoid pushing trades.

## Anti-references

Avoid day-trading terminals, loud stock-ticker dashboards, casino-like red/green flashing, promise-of-profit language, and generic finance SaaS hero styling.

## Design Principles

- Lead with holding logic and risk context, not price excitement.
- Explain the trigger before asking for attention.
- Make concentration risk visible without shaming the user.
- Prefer calm, compact desktop utility over decorative spectacle.
- Keep financial language neutral and non-promissory.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced motion, keep text legible in compact transparent windows, and avoid relying on color alone for risk states.
